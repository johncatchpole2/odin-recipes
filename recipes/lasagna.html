Lasagna

Recipes Lasagna Prepared

This recipe takes inspiration from classic Italian Lasagna dishes whilst incorporating a healthier take through the use of healthier ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Large onion(250g), finely chopped
  • 320g Carrots, finely chopped
  • 2 Celery sticks(140g), finely chopped
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 500g 5% Beef Mince
  • 3 Large garlic olives, finely grated
  • 400g Can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1½ Tsp vegetable bouillon powder
  • 400ml Semi-skimmed milk
  • 30g Wholemeal flour
  • Generous pinch of nutmeg
  • 6 Sheets wholemeal lasagna (125g)
  • 25g Finely grated parmesan
