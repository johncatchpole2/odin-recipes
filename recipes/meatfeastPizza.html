Meatfeast Pizza

Recipes Meatfeast Pizza Prepared

A homemade slim crust pizza with a healthy take on a greasy classic

Ingredients

Serves 1

  • 1 piece of Lo-Dough
  • 20g of Ham
  • 20g of Cooked chicken breast
  • 25g of Lean spicy mince, cooked
  • 15g of Pepperoni
  • 100g of Mozzarella
  • 2 Tablespoons of passata
  • A twist of black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220Â°C/428 F.
  2. Spread the passata across the Lo-Dough in a thin layer, right up to the edge.
  3. Spread the grated cheese on top of the passata and add the ham, chicken, beef and Pepperoni. Put the pizza in the oven for 7 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
  4. Serve with a crack of black pepper on top.
